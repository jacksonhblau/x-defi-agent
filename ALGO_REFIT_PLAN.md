# Algo-Refit Plan — May 2026

This is the change spec for retuning the DeFi X Poster agent to fit the May 2026 X ranking algorithm and Jackson's three voice constraints. See `CHANGELOG_ALGO_REFIT.md` for the executed work and `docs/x_algorithm_2026_signals.md`, `docs/higgsfield_integration.md`, `docs/canva_integration.md` for the supporting briefs.

## The three constraints

1. Every post ships with at least one generated media asset — Higgsfield image/video for editorial content, OR a Canva-rendered brand template with real issuer logos for data-led content. High-materiality posts may carry both.
2. Voice is first-person specific, not third-person abstract.
3. No hallucinated personal actions — opinions only, defended with evidence from the story brief or the `personal_facts.json` ledger.
